Dedos de Toledo
This game is a recreation of several real, historical dice games mentioned in the 13th century Book of Games by Alfonso X of Castile. Thus far I have recreated 3 of the games mentioned: Triga, Panquist, and Riffa. This was my first real project getting to use programming to get a deeper look at lived history. Plus, it's actually quite fun!
